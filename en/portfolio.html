Portfolio

Year: 2023

My portfolio is a display that merges minimalist elegance with modern functionality. Developed with tools like HTML, CSS, and a bit of JavaScript. I implemented the library RoughNotation to create a highlighting and underlining effect on keywords, adding a distinctive touch to the visual presentation of my content.

The fluid and minimalist design offers a visually appealing and easy-to-navigate experience. Every element, from the layout of information to the choice of colors, has been carefully selected to convey professionalism and creativity.

Furthermore, the platform has been optimized for intuitive user interaction, providing a seamless browsing experience. I used tools like Squoosh to optimize images and Shots.so to design mockups, ensuring fast loading and aesthetic presentation of projects.
