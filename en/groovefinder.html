GrooveFinder

Year: 2024

As a passionate DJ and music enthusiast, I decided to create GrooveFinder to meet my own need for discovering new tracks and music genres quickly and easily. This web application utilizes React technology and the powerful Spotify API to offer an unparalleled music discovery experience.

With GrooveFinder, users can explore a wide variety of music genres, from indie to hip-hop, and play song previews directly within the app. Best of all? With just one click, they can access direct links to Spotify to enjoy the full song.

GrooveFinder is designed to make musical discovery more exciting and accessible than ever before. Additionally, with an intuitive and user-friendly interface, GrooveFinder is suitable for users of all ages and levels of experience.
